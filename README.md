## About

I build production React applications, EVM smart contracts, APIs, dashboards, automation, and AI-assisted workflows for DeFi/RWA platforms, protocol teams, finance-adjacent products, and private trading infrastructure.

I work best where product ambiguity and technical complexity meet: early platforms, DeFi/RWA products, trading tools, internal dashboards, protocol integrations, and AI-assisted operator workflows.

- **For founders** — I can own the build when hiring a full team is too early: scope, UX, frontend, backend, contracts, deployment, and iteration.
- **For dApp teams** — I plug into protocol and product teams to ship wallet flows, contract integrations, dashboards, admin tools, tests, and deployments.
- **For finance-backed projects** — I understand DeFi, RWA, and trading constraints, and build with access control, traceability, verification, and operations in mind.
